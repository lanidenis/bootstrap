The Founder
& Her Mission ...

   Francesca Denis is a recent MBA graduate and millennial entrepreneur. She loves touching hearts during holiday season through cooking, baking, and giving. However, this year after constantly being asked for her Crémas,  she decided to blend her creative thinking, love for celebration, entrepreneur, and cooking skills into one !


   The name “Férier” was derived from the phrase “Jou Férier”, a retro term from the French and Haitian Creole language, that was used to refer to holiday season ; More specifically, the days taken off for vacation during national holidays. Most everyone enjoys holiday season as it brings time off from work, quality time with family and friends, gifts, delicious food, warmth, happiness, and most importantly the feeling of love. Férier aims to be a symbol of this holiday warmth and continue to spread love, peace, and happiness through the creation of different delicacies and delights !  


   Being of haitian descent, her mission is to bring the authenticity of celebration and holiday vacation from the Haitian culture, spin a little twist, and speak  to the new generation diaspora in hopes of maintaining Haitian tradition. By bringing together the old and the new, she hopes to bridge the gap , and encourage spread of the notion that "no matter where we go, we must never forget the mother land". 12-08-34
